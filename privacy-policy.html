Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Leap Over (also referred to as "Bookiepia", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you register, we collect email address, password (stored securely), and optionally display name and profile photo. If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and basic profile information as permitted by Google.
  • Usage and preferences: App settings such as theme (light/dark), language, playback speed, sleep timer, and voice preferences. Listening progress, bookmarks, playlists, and download preferences may be stored locally and, where applicable, synced to our servers to support your experience across devices.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, app version, and similar technical data for compatibility, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Analytics and crash data: Aggregated usage events (e.g., screen views, feature usage) and crash reports to improve stability and product decisions. Analytics can be disabled in app settings where offered.
  • Subscription and payment-related data: Subscription status and purchase information are processed by our payment/subscription provider (e.g., Google Play Billing). We do not store your full payment card details; our provider (e.g., RevenueCat) may receive anonymized or hashed identifiers to manage entitlements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features (audiobooks, playback, downloads, sync).
  • Authenticate you and manage your account.
  • Process and manage subscriptions and premium features.
  • Send you push notifications (e.g., reminders, new content) if you have granted permission and opted in.
  • Analyze usage patterns and fix crashes to improve performance and user experience.
  • Comply with applicable laws and enforce our terms of service.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may collect or process data on our behalf:

  • Google / Firebase: Authentication (including Google Sign-In), cloud database (Firestore), push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging), analytics, and crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics). Their privacy practices apply: Google Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and entitlement checks. See RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play: In-app purchases and subscription billing. Subject to Google Privacy Policy and Google Play terms.
  • Cloudflare: Secure delivery of encrypted audio content (e.g., via Workers). See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for advertising or marketing.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store account and synced data on secure servers (e.g., Firebase). Some data (e.g., preferences, offline content) is stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we encourage you to use a strong password and keep your device secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can access and update account and profile information from within the App.
  • You may disable analytics where the App provides an opt-out option.
  • You can disable push notifications in your device settings or in-app notification settings.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process such requests in accordance with applicable law.
  • Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (e.g., access, portability, rectification, restriction, objection) under laws such as GDPR or CCPA.

6. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or higher age where required by law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. International Data

Your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to such transfer and processing in accordance with this policy.

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we may provide additional notice (e.g., in-app or by email) where required by law.
