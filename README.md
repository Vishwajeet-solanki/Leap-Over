## What is Leap Over?

Leap Over is a reading and learning app that helps you explore big ideas in less time. Each title is distilled into a focused experience you can **read** or **listen to**—perfect for commutes, coffee breaks, or a quiet evening when you still want to feel like you moved forward.

Pick up where you left off, follow a clear path through topics that matter to you, and set goals so your home screen reflects *your* journey—not a generic feed. Challenges and achievements turn consistency into something you can actually see and celebrate.

We built Leap Over for people who want **depth without overwhelm**: a calm, modern home for curiosity, habit, and real progress.

---

## What’s inside

Leap Over ships with a **curated catalog** you can start exploring right away:

- **19 hand-picked titles** — distilled ideas you can read or listen to, built for busy days  
- **6 topic areas** — browse by the kind of growth you want, not endless scrolling  

**Those topics span:** Business & career · Happiness · Money & investment · Productivity · Self-growth · Spirituality  

We keep **growing the library** and deepening each journey—so expect more to discover over time.

---

## What you can do

- **Browse and search** across categories to find the idea that fits your mood or your goals  
- **Read and listen** with audio and playlists so learning fits how you move through the day  
- **Save what matters** with bookmarks and highlights you can return to anytime  
- **Stay motivated** with challenges and achievements that reward showing up  
- **Control your experience** with downloads and notification settings for focus and offline moments  
- **Go further** with an optional **Pro** plan that unlocks more of the full Leap Over experience  

---

## Who it’s for

Leap Over is for **students**, **professionals**, and **curious readers** who want smarter summaries, steadier habits, and a little structure—without losing the freedom to explore what interests *you*.
